Drum Code Challenge

  • TunePad uses numbers to represent different notes and drum sounds.
  • Try clicking on each different drum to hear how it sounds.
  • Pay attention to the note numbers for different sounds like kick drums, snare drums, and hi-hats.

Try changing the code in the three drum cells below to make your own original beat.
You can play one cell at a time or play them all at the same time to hear how they sound together.

/# play four kick drum sounds /playNote(1) /playNote(1) /playNote(1) /playNote(1) / /# play two snare drum sounds on beats 2 and 4 /rest(1) /playNote(2) /rest(1) /playNote(2) / /# play hi-hat sounds /for i in range(4): / rest(0.5) / playNote(4, beats = 0.5) /
